Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 4, 2026

Tamras Co. ("we", "us", or "our") operates the MACRA mobile application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

1. Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Profile Information: Name, weight, height, age, gender, and activity level — used solely for calculating your macro goals.
  • Meal Data: Food names, calories, and macronutrient values you log.
  • Goal Preferences: Goal type and activity level.

Information Collected Automatically

  • Apple Health Data: With your permission, we read step count and active energy. This data never leaves your device.
  • Subscription Status: Verified through Apple's StoreKit. We do not process payments directly.

Information We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect analytics, tracking data, advertising identifiers, or location data. There are no third-party SDKs in MACRA.

2. Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. Nothing is transmitted to external servers.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with any third parties.

4. Data Retention

Data persists on your device while the App is installed. Uninstalling removes all data. We maintain no server-side backups.

5. Your Rights

You have full control: all data is visible in-app, and uninstalling deletes everything. Health permissions can be revoked in iOS Settings.

6. Children's Privacy

MACRA is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from minors.

7. Security

Your data is protected by iOS encryption and app sandboxing. Data never leaves your device.

8. Changes

We may update this policy. Changes will be posted within the App and on this page.
