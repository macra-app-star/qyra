# Privacy Policy

**MACRA — Privacy Policy**
*Last updated: March 4, 2026*

Tamras Co. ("we", "us", or "our") operates the MACRA mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the App.

## 1. Information We Collect

### Information You Provide
- **Profile Information:** During onboarding, you may provide your name, weight, height, age, gender, and activity level. This information is used solely to calculate your personalized macro nutrient goals.
- **Meal Data:** Food names, calorie counts, and macronutrient values you log through the App.
- **Goal Preferences:** Your selected goal type (cut, maintain, bulk) and activity level.

### Information Collected Automatically
- **Apple Health Data:** With your explicit permission, the App reads step count and active energy burned from Apple Health. This data is only used to display activity metrics in the App and is never transmitted off your device.
- **Subscription Status:** We verify your subscription status through Apple's StoreKit framework. We do not process or store payment information directly.

### Information We Do NOT Collect
- We do not collect analytics or usage data.
- We do not use third-party tracking or advertising SDKs.
- We do not collect device identifiers for advertising purposes.
- We do not collect your location.

## 2. How Your Data Is Stored

All personal data, meal logs, and health information are stored **locally on your device** using Apple's SwiftData framework. Your data remains in an App Group container on your device and is not transmitted to external servers.

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide exclusively to:
- Calculate personalized macro nutrient goals
- Display your daily nutrition tracking data
- Show your activity metrics from Apple Health
- Provide weekly insights and streak tracking

## 4. Data Sharing

**We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with any third parties.**

The only external service interaction is with Apple's App Store for subscription verification, which is handled entirely by Apple's frameworks.

## 5. Data Retention

Your data persists on your device as long as the App is installed. Uninstalling the App removes all locally stored data. We do not maintain backups of your data on any server.

## 6. Your Rights

You have full control over your data:
- **Access:** All your data is visible within the App.
- **Deletion:** Uninstalling the App deletes all stored data.
- **Health Data:** You can revoke Apple Health permissions at any time through your device's Settings > Privacy & Security > Health.

## 7. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

## 8. Security

Your data is protected by iOS's built-in encryption and sandboxing. Data stored in the App Group container is encrypted at rest by the operating system. We do not implement additional network-based security measures because your data never leaves your device.

## 9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the new Privacy Policy within the App or on our website. Your continued use of the App after such changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
